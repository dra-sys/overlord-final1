About Animate

Animate is a well regarded chain store offering the latest (and usually priciest) anime and manga merch. The newest merch is going to be expensive, but discounted items can be found. Especially figures which are constantly rolling out for popular characters. Figures that you see in UFO catchers can usually be found here for under ¥1,000.
